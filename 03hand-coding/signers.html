7 Common Chemical Allergens and where they're found

Ethyl Acrylate and HEMA

  1. Nail products
  2. Dental products
  3. Paint/ink products
  4. Metal products

Tea Tree Oil

  1. Tea based beauty product
  2. Baby Products

Methyldibromo Glutaronitrile (MDBGN)

  1. Sunscreen
  2. Paint products
  3. Resin/glue products
  4. Toilet Tissue products

Glyceral Monothioglycolate

  1. Hair products

Decyl Glucoside

  1. Cosmetic products
  2. Shampoo/shower products
  3. Lotion

Limonene and Hyperoxides of Linalool

  1. Fragrant products
  2. Food products
  3. Anything labeled as parfum or fragrance
